Requirements

There are no prerequisites for being eligible to hire a management agency as long as you are willing to further your growth as an artist. We are looking for fresh and young talent to help expand their reach and network in the industry.

Why do you need us?

Managing art is like managing a business. With the industry becoming more entrepreneurial and artist- centric, it is often not easy to manage the in numerous activities while making music and being the face of your brand. From making contacts and maintaining PR relations, to enhancing one's brand image and marketing the art; a growing artist needs to focus on establishing themselves as a presence within the online as well as offline network. That's where we come in as buffers, and make it a single step ladder for you to focus on your art while we pave a way for you to slide into the desired community.
