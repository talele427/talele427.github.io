about

Ramil is an independent artist based out of Mumbai. Growing up in a house full of artists and having a knack for playing guitar from a very early age, he discovered music as a way of expressing himself. His compositions reflect his love for acoustic melodies, metaphoric lines, and pleasant tunes.

Singles from his new EP- Gulmohar, have been appreciated friends and critics alike, and have garnered a lot of attention over various media. Ramil is now looking forward to exploring new sounds and creating new stuff in the coming year.

If there's anything that can be seen throughout Ramil's musical journey, it is a unique brand value that combines smooth vocals and sweet harmonies into contemporary rhythms.
